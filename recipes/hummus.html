Hummus

  • Two cups of cooked chick peas, from a can or from scratch
  • 1 tablespoon Tahini
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • salt to taste
  • cumin to taste
  • lemon juice to taste

Steps

  1. Drain the chick peas, saving the liquid, and puree in food processor until smooth
  2. To food processor add tahini, salt, pepper,cumin and lemon juice.
  3. Puree all ingredients until smooth.
  4. If hummus is too dry, add some of the liquid from the chick peas until desired consistency is reached.
  5. Serve with pita, raw vegetables or chips. Lasts about two days refrigerated.
