Hot Spiced Cider

Ingredients

  • 1/2 gallon apple cider
  • 8 whole cloves
  • 3 sticks cinnamon
  • juice and peels of two large oranges. Peels should be zested.

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, combine all ingredients
  2. Over medium heat, heat cider slowly until it begins to steam. If it boils, it becomes bitter
  3. Strain cider into mugs and serve hot, or chill to reheat the next day. Lasts about two days.
