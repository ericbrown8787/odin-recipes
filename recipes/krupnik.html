Krupnik

Ingredients

  • 1lb of your favorite honey.
  • 1 750ml bottle of vodka
  • 8 whole cloves
  • 3 sticks cinnamon
  • 12 peppercorns

Steps

  1. In a large, sanitized mixing bowl, stir the honey into the vodka until completely dissolved
  2. Place cloves, cinnamon and peppercorns in a large, airtight container such as a mason jar
  3. Pour the honey and vodka solution over the spices in the mason jar.
  4. Shake the mason jar vigorously for at least a minute.
  5. Allow the krupnik to infuse for at least two weeks.
  6. Strain the krupnik into a liquor bottle using a coffee filter or cheesecloth in order to remove any sediment.
  7. Krupnik can be served at room temperature or chilled, and will keep pretty much indefinitely.
